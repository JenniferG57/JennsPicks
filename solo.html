Solo-Leveling

Manhwa
Solo-Leveling
Creator
Chu-Gong
Rating:
8/10
Genre:
Genre:Action, Adventure, Fantasy
Description:10 years ago, after “the Gate” that connected the real world with the monster world opened, some of the ordinary, everyday people received the power to hunt monsters within the Gate. They are known as "Hunters". However, not all Hunters are powerful. My name is Sung Jin-Woo, an E-rank Hunter. This is the story of Sung Jin-Woo and his journey to become the world strongest S-Rank Hunter.
My Opinion:
The art is incredible, especially during fighting scenes. The story has good progression within the story line and theres always another antagonist waiting to follow up the last one which calls for entertaining fight scenes. I would mostly reccomend this to someone whos geniunly intrested in action stories as it is a long manhwa.
